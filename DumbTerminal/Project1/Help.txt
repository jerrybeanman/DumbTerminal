This is a application for serial port communication. 
--------------------------------------------------------------------
To enter connect mode, select 'Connect' on the menu and 
enter the appropriate corresponding parameters in order 
for the two machines to communicate.
In this mode, two computers connected through the serial 
port will be able to perform asynchrounous read/write. 
Characers recieved from the serial port will be displayed in Green, 
and characters sent will be displayed in Yellow. 
Additionlly, the program will also process special characters 
such as carriage return and backspace
--------------------------------------------------------------------
To exit the connect mode, select the 'Exit' menu item.
